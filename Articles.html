Forest Of Rituals — The First VPanda Studio Game

Posted by VPanda Studio | Nov 10, 2025

Introducing Forest Of Rituals — a psychological survival game set in an eerie, abandoned forest. Built with Unreal Engine 5, enhanced by Megascans, Niagara System, and other cutting-edge technologies, it’s the first major project from VPanda Studio.

The game explores the limits of human psyche, ritualistic symbolism, and environmental storytelling. Every step in the forest hides a mystery — and a consequence.
