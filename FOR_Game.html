Forest Of Rituals

Forest Of Rituals is a dark, atmospheric horror game designed to immerse players into a terrifying forest environment. Explore, survive, and uncover secrets as you navigate through a world of horror and mystery.

Trailer

If the video does not load, watch it on YouTube.

Game History

Long ago, the forest was a sacred ground where rituals shaped the balance between life and death. Over centuries, darkness crept into its depths, corrupting the spirits and creatures that once thrived. As the player, you awaken in this cursed forest, where each decision influences the fate of both the living and the dead. Can you uncover the truth hidden in the shadows?

Game Features

Open World Exploration
Dynamic Horror Events
Mystery & Puzzle Solving
Immersive Audio & Visuals
Multiple Endings
Unique Creature Encounters
