Forest Of Rituals

Forest Of Rituals is a dark, atmospheric horror game designed to immerse players into a terrifying forest environment. Explore, survive, and uncover secrets as you navigate through a world of horror and mystery. Created by VPanda & VPanda Studio, this is the studio's first project, developed using the powerful Unreal Engine.

Game History

As a detective returning home late one evening, you receive a chilling call from your superior: a group of teenagers has gone missing after setting up camp deep in the forest. Investigating the scene, you quickly realize this is no ordinary disappearance. Strange, paranormal occurrences manifest around you, and an ancient, awakened force lurks in the shadows. The dark rituals once performed in these woods have left a vengeful imprint upon the land, twisting its creatures and spirits into relentless guardians of the cursed forest. Every step you take brings you closer to the truth and further into a nightmare from which escape may be impossible.
