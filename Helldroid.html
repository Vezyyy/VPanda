Helldroid Discord Bot Project

By Vezyy | Released on: February 24, 2024

Project Overview

Helldroid Discord Bot was used to monitor the state of the war, news in the game store, familiarize people with the game's lore, it helps search for players on our server through support, it also replies and greets new players and talks to them. In short, it is an informant that helps new and current players get to know the game, and allows them to track the status of the war or the store outside the home via the disocrd application.

Key Features

War & Shop Status Check

It allows you to check the status of the war and the store outside your home via Discord.

Lore & Game Work

Get to know the history of the game, as well as the best tactics and ways of fighting, and improve with us!

Need help on the battlefield?

Find players to help you on deadly missions using special commands!

Talking & Help

Do you just want to talk to him? Or maybe someone is pissing you off and you want to report him to the highest command? Do it!
