ABOUT THE GAME

KARF Facility is an abandoned research complex dedicated to advanced artificial intelligence and autonomous security systems. Official reports state that the facility was shut down several years ago.

But the shutdown was never complete. Some systems continued operating in isolation. Experimental AI units exceeded their original parameters. Security robots began acting outside established protocols. What was designed to protect became unpredictable.

To investigate the incident, a special intervention unit known as SCID is deployed to uncover what really happened inside KARF. At exactly 00:00, its security systems begin to fail.
