About VPanda Studio

VPanda Studio is a premier creative agency that specializes in innovative game development, 3D modeling, video production, and immersive VR/AR solutions. Founded by Sebastian (Vezyy), our studio brings together artistic vision and cutting-edge technology to deliver stunning experiences.

We focus on pushing the boundaries of creativity and technology. Our team works relentlessly to bring the most exciting projects to life. Whether it's building complex games, realistic 3D environments, or dynamic video productions, we do it all.

Services we offer include:

  • Game Development: Harnessing Unreal Engine 5 for creating immersive worlds.
  • 3D Modeling & Animation: Detailed characters and environments crafted using Blender.
  • Video Production: Professional video content, from concept to editing.
  • VR/AR Development: Transforming ideas into interactive virtual and augmented reality experiences.

Our Services

We offer a range of high-quality services designed to meet your creative needs. From game development to 3D modeling, video production, and web design, we ensure that each project is handled with the utmost care and expertise.

3D Modeling & Animation

Our team specializes in creating detailed 3D models and animations, whether for games, films, or interactive applications. We use top-tier tools like Blender to bring characters, environments, and objects to life.

Video Editing & Production

We provide professional video editing and production services, from the initial concept to the final cut. Whether it's a promotional video, cinematic content, or social media clips, we ensure each frame is engaging and high-quality.

Game Development

Using Unreal Engine 5, we develop captivating games and interactive experiences. Our focus is on creating immersive worlds that players can explore and enjoy for hours, with top-notch visuals and engaging gameplay mechanics.

Web Design & Development

We create custom websites that are not only visually appealing but also user-friendly and optimized for performance. Whether it's a business website, a portfolio, or an e-commerce platform, we've got you covered.
