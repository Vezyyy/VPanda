Privacy Policy

Effective Date: December 17, 2024

Welcome to VPanda! We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines the types of data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your personal data.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of personal and non-personal information:

  • Personal Information: Full name, email address, contact number, and address.
  • Account Information: Username, password, and other account-related details.
  • Technical Information: IP address, browser type, operating system, and device information.
  • Usage Information: Information about your interactions with our website, such as visited pages and time spent on the site.
  • Location Information: Approximate location details based on IP address.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information in the following ways:

  • To enhance your user experience.
  • To improve our services and develop new features.
  • To communicate updates, news, and offers.
  • To ensure security and prevent technical issues.
  • To comply with legal and regulatory obligations.

3. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal data. However, we may share your information with:

  • Service Providers: Third-party companies that help us with hosting, analytics, and support.
  • Legal Authorities: In compliance with legal requirements or court orders.
  • Affiliates and Partners: To improve our service offerings and collaborate on projects.

4. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies to:

  • Personalize and improve your experience on our website.
  • Analyze website traffic and usage patterns.
  • Provide targeted advertisements.

By continuing to use our website, you consent to our use of cookies. You can control cookie preferences through your browser settings.

5. Data Security

We adopt advanced security measures to protect your personal information, including:

  • Data Encryption: Ensuring your information is securely transmitted and stored.
  • Access Control: Restricting access to your data to authorized personnel only.
  • Regular Security Audits: Continuous monitoring and compliance checks.

6. Your Privacy Rights

As a user, you have the following rights:

  • Access Rights: Request access to your personal information.
  • Correction Rights: Request updates or corrections to inaccurate personal data.
  • Deletion Rights: Request the removal of your data from our systems.
  • Objection Rights: Object to the processing of your personal information.
  • Consent Withdrawal: Opt-out of receiving communications at any time.

7. Third-Party Links

Our website may include links to third-party websites. VPanda is not responsible for their privacy policies or practices. We encourage you to review their privacy policies before sharing personal information.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our services, legal requirements, or operational improvements. Any updates will be published on this page with an updated revision date.

If you have any questions about our Privacy Policy, Contact Us.
