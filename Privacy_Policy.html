Privacy Policy

Welcome to VPanda | Vezyy. We value your privacy and want you to feel secure while using our website. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and what rights you have regarding your data.

1. Data Controller

The data controller of your personal information is [Sebastian Gajger], operating under the name VPanda | Vezyy.

2. What Data Do We Collect?

We collect the following personal data:

  • Name - to address you correctly in communication.
  • Email address - to contact you for support or inquiries.
  • Information from the contact form - such as your request details.
  • Technical data - such as IP address, browser type, and cookies.

3. How Do We Use Your Data?

Your data may be used for the following purposes:

  • Responding to inquiries: We use your contact details to respond to your requests.
  • Personalization: Tailoring the website experience for you.
  • Traffic analysis: To improve the website's functionality.

4. Legal Basis for Processing Data

The processing of your personal data is based on:

  • Your consent: E.g., subscribing to newsletters or agreeing to cookies.
  • Contract necessity: E.g., responding to inquiries.
  • Legitimate interest: Analyzing site traffic.

5. Data Retention

Your data will be retained as long as necessary to fulfill its purpose, or until you withdraw consent.

6. Sharing Data

Your data will not be shared with third parties unless required by law.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data: Request a copy of your data.
  • Rectify incorrect data: Request to correct any inaccuracies.
  • Erase data: Request deletion under certain conditions.
  • Restrict data processing: Limit how your data is used.
  • Data portability: Request your data in a readable format.
  • Object to processing: Object to data processing for certain purposes.

To exercise your rights, please contact us via Discord at [vezyy].

The website uses cookies to improve service quality. You can manage your preferences through browser settings.

9. Changes to the Privacy Policy

We may update this policy. Changes will be posted on this page.
