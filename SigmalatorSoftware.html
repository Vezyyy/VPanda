Sigmalator Software Project

By Vezyy | Released on: July 5, 2024

Project Overview

Sigmalator is an innovative tool developed by SigmaTech, a part of VPanda, to streamline and enhance the recruitment process within our community. It provides an intuitive interface, robust performance, and a wide range of features tailored to efficiently onboard new members.

SigmaTech and BTB (Behind The Blackwall) are integral parts of VPanda, dedicated to delivering cutting-edge solutions in recruitment technology.

Key Features

Streamlined Recruitment Process

Sigmalator simplifies and accelerates the recruitment process, making it easier to onboard new team members efficiently.

Customizable Onboarding Workflows

Create and customize onboarding workflows tailored to your team's specific needs and roles.

Automated Task Assignment

Automate task assignments and notifications to ensure smooth integration of new members into your team.

Collaborative Tools

Facilitate collaboration among team members with integrated communication and task management tools.
